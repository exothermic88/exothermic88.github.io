    Installing NCOS with Calamares

    The Calamares installer simplifies the NCOS installation process with an intuitive, graphical interface. Follow the steps below to install NCOS on your system.

    Step 1: Boot into the Live Environment

    • Insert the bootable USB drive containing the NCOS ISO into your computer.
    • Restart the system and boot from the USB drive. (Refer to the Pre-Installation Guide if needed.)
    • Once the system boots, you'll enter the NCOS live environment.

    Step 2: Launch the Calamares Installer

    • On the desktop or in the system menu, locate and click the "Install NCOS" icon to launch the Calamares installer.
    • The Calamares installer interface will guide you through the process step by step.

    Step 3: Choose Your Language

    • On the first screen, select your preferred language for the installation.
    • Click "Next" to proceed.

    Step 4: Select Your Region and Time Zone

    • Choose your region and time zone from the map or dropdown menu.
    • Click "Next" to continue.

    Step 5: Configure Your Keyboard Layout

    • Select your keyboard layout and variant (e.g., US, UK, or other).
    • Use the test field to ensure your keyboard is set up correctly.
    • Click "Next" when you're ready.

    Step 6: Partition Your Disk

    The partitioning step allows you to define how NCOS will be installed on your storage device. You have two main options:

    Option 1: Erase Disk

    • Select "Erase Disk" to automatically partition your entire disk for NCOS.
    • (Optional) Check the box to enable encryption and set a passphrase for added security.

    Option 2: Manual Partitioning

    • Choose "Manual Partitioning" for advanced users who want to create and configure partitions manually.

    • Ensure you create at least:

      • A root (/) partition with sufficient space.
      • (Optional) A separate /home partition for personal files.
      • A swap partition (if required by your system).

    • Click "Next" once your partition scheme is ready.

    Step 7: Set Your User Account

    • Enter a username, hostname, and password for your account.
    • (Optional) Check the box to log in automatically without a password.
    • Click "Next" to proceed.

    Step 8: Review Installation Summary

    • Review the installation summary, which includes your selected options.
    • Confirm that everything is correct.
    • Click "Install" to begin the installation.

    Step 9: Wait for the Installation to Complete

    • The installer will now copy files, configure the system, and install NCOS on your disk.
    • This process may take a few minutes.

    Step 10: Restart Your System

    • Once the installation is complete, click "Restart Now" and remove the USB drive when prompted.
    • Your system will reboot into your newly installed NCOS.

    You're all set! Welcome to NCOS. 🎉 Explore and enjoy your new operating system. If you encounter any issues, visit the Troubleshooting page for assistance.
